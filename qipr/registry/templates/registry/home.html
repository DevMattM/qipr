What is the mission of Quality Improvement?

UF Health will strive to become a high reliability organization (HRO) by putting in place consistent processes that ensure the delivery of timely, patient-centered, accurate, error-free and effective care. Through our drive to become a HRO, we will improve our performance in everything we do. High reliabilty establishes the trust that is the foundation of the relationship between care-givers and patients.
